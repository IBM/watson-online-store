    ByMarcon January 10, 2017
    Size: 12 D(M) US|Color: Black/White/Cool Grey|Verified Purchase
    2 main issues. Firstly, right out of the box the stitched nike decal on the shoes were not white, they were a yellow white, as if the decals were shoved into a small room with a heavy smoker for a few months then placed on the shoes. This is not an exaggeration, the logo is supposed to be white (as is in the picture) and looks old and sickly. The white outline on the bottom of the shoes, however, were white, as pictured. This only helped make the logo's off coloring even more noticable.

    Secondly, these shoes are uncomfortable for me. Typically, if I choose a shoe, if it's size 11, I need to order it wide. But size 12-13 fits fine normally. Nike's shoes are usually pretty narrow, but a size 12 is usually just fine. These shoes were too tight and rubbed against my little and big toes so much it gave me calluses and I was nearly limping at the end of a shift at work. This issue, though, I'll blame on myself, I should've been more cautious with my size selection.

    Overall, I wouldn't recommend these. The sickly yellow coloring alone, that makes these shoes look like hand me downs that have been in storage for a couple years is enough to turn me off of the seller.
