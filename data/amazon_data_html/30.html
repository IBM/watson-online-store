    1.0 out of 5 starsSoles Bald In 5 Weeks--Garbage
    ByderwoodProgrammeron August 15, 2016
    Size: 10 D(M) US|Color: Black/Anthracite/Black|Verified Purchase
    These are very comfortable sneakers. I was pleased when I got them.

    Today marks 5 weeks to the day I got them, and as I put them on this morning, I happened to look at the soles--I was shocked to see the tread is worn to baldenss both on the heels and ball of foot. And yes, I mean where there was tread, it is worn to smooth.

    I still can't believe it. So I calculated exactly the mileage I put on them; I do walk a lot, and I run 3 miles 4 times a week. I have put no more than 178 miles on these sneakers--and likely closer to 160 miles.

    That is just unacceptable for a $100 sneaker--170 miles and the things are worn out? 5 weeks of use?

    Nike is taking advantage of us customers; I am quite sure the rubber used for the sneakers is engineered to wear out quickly. There is no other explanation.

    Even if you walk a mere mile per day--you won't even get a half year out of these things.

    So, if you are a couch potato that walks a few feet every day, these are great shoes. If you do any sort of excercising, or walk even the most modest of distances, these things are a total rip off.
