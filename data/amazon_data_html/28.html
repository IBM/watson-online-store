Product description

Part of the Nike Air family, the Nike Air Max 1 Essential Men's running shoe is a throwback combination of the Air Max with a retro look perfect for street style, comfort, and a classic look you expect in a Nike Air Max shoe. It has an upper made of leather and mesh for both durability and breath-ability and it was designed to provide maximum impact protection during repetitive landings, and feature less midsole material and larger-volume airbags for advanced cushioning and impact protection made with premium materials for style and durability along with lighter weight and maximum cushioning you can see. The full-length PU midsole and visible Air-Sole unit in heel provides maximum cushioning and impact protection, and therubber outsole combined with a Waffle pattern add traction and durability.
