4.0 out of 5 starsI ordered a Small in black with blue stripes and it fits great! I am 5'11"
ByHarry Linon April 17, 2015
Size: Small|Color: Black/Air Force Blue/Black|Verified Purchase
When I read customer reviews of products such as these, I really want to know one thing: how does it fit? Because you can't try 'em on over the interwebs. So here's my review addressing that question:

I ordered a Small in black with blue stripes and it fits great! I am 5'11", 150 lbs., 30.5' waist, 32' inseam. That should give you my relevant dimensions. Basically, I'm thin but not hipster-thin.

After machine-washing in cold and tumble-drying in low, they shrank by maybe 1%. The waist is perfect for me and doesn't require cinching the inner drawcord. So, if you buy a Small but your waist is, say, 32", you might find them too tight. The length is on almost on long side for me. There is a tiny bit of bunching at the break, but when I stretch or sit down, the hem is fine and doesn't show off a bunch of ankle or sock. Also, the hem is very tapered, almost like tights. The bottom half hugs the calf and shin. If you buy a Small but your inseam is, say, 30", you'll get a lot of bunching at the break. If your inseam is, say, 34", you'll have some ankle showing even when standing up straight.

They look good. They're a bit warmer than I'd like (I live in southern California) but that's okay, the material is the material. Bottom line, if you can figure out your sizing based on what I shared about mine, you'll get a good-looking trainer pant that fits slim and tapered.
