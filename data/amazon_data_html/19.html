In terms of overall comfort of the material I would say Brooks Brothers and Banana Republic are may favorite of the bunch. The shirts feel comfortable without feeling flimsy or heavy. I will say that most of the collars on my other t-shirts lose shape over time, which is the big selling point for this t-shirt.

    The Fruit of the Loom collar will hold up longer as there is an elastic band around the collar. However, this also means the t-shirt collar is snug around the neck. I prefer a bit of a lower collar as I don't like to have the white t-shirt showing through. In terms of comfort of the material I would say the Fruit of the Loom ranks somewhere between Old Navy and Gap. Last but not least is the fit of the shirt itself. I ordered a Small and I would say of all the brands I've tried this is the truest in terms of size as most others are a bit loose for a Small.
